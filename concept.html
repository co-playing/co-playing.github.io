The Sound of Water

How do we perceive the sound of water?

Physically, it is the vibration caused by water molecules colliding with each other, transmitted to our eardrums as sound.

Naturally, it is the sound of rain, oceans, and riversâ€”born from the interaction between the sun and the Earth, as water circulates.

Historically, it is the sound of water that brought people together, forming settlements and shaping civilizations through advancements in agriculture and technology.

How does a machine perceive the sound of water? How does a machine see and hear this interaction?

This piece creates an interactive space where the sound of water, made by a human, is heard and seen by a machine, allowing them to perform together.
